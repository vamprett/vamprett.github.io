Books Design

What does it mean?

Book Design refers to the general artwork that is the cover of the book. For it is very important to be able to convey the message of the book in just a drawing.

How does it work?

It is not done without knowledge upon the book and without knowing what the book is about. The usual process, for me, takes about two weeks, depending on the week, in which I cover: character description, plot, important elements, specific key designs and client requirements.

What if I am not satisfied?

That is not a problem at all! At first, I present you sketches to see if your concepts are the same as I understood them. It is an on-going process that requires communication on both parts. Happy to help all the time!
